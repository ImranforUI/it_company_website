# TechSolutions Website - User Guide

## Welcome to TechSolutions

**Purpose**: TechSolutions is a modern IT company website showcasing innovative technology solutions and services for business growth and digital transformation.

**Access**: Public website - no login required

## Using Your Website

### Navigating the Website

The website features a clean navigation structure at the top:

1. **Click "Home"** to return to the main page
2. **Click "About Us"** to learn about the company
3. **Click "Services"** to view all IT solutions offered
4. **Click "How It Works"** to understand the process
5. **Click "Testimonials"** to read client reviews
6. **Click "Contact"** to reach out
7. **Click "Get Started"** (orange button) to jump to the contact form

### Main Sections

**Hero Section**: The top banner introduces TechSolutions with a compelling headline "Transform Your Business with Cutting-Edge Technology" and two buttons: "Get Started" and "Learn More."

**Our Services**: Six service cards display: Web Development, Mobile Apps, Cloud Solutions, Cybersecurity, Performance Optimization, and Consulting. Each card shows what the service includes.

**About Us**: Learn about the company's 8+ years of experience, team expertise, and commitment to customer success. Features a "Get in Touch" button for inquiries.

**How It Works**: A three-step process explains the journey: Consultation → Planning & Design → Development & Launch.

**Statistics**: Animated counters show 150+ Projects, 120+ Happy Clients, 8+ Years Experience, and 45+ Team Members.

**Client Testimonials**: Scroll through 5-star reviews from satisfied clients using the left/right arrow buttons.

**Contact Form**: Fill in your Name, Email, Phone, Subject, and Message, then click "Send Message" to submit an inquiry.

### Floating WhatsApp Button

A green WhatsApp icon appears in the bottom-right corner. Click it to open WhatsApp and send a message directly to TechSolutions.

## Managing Your Website

### Settings & Customization

Use the Management UI to customize your website:

- **Settings → General**: Update website name and logo
- **Settings → Domains**: Modify your website URL or add custom domains
- **Dashboard**: View visitor analytics and website performance

### Updating Content

To modify website content (company name, services, testimonials, contact info):

1. Contact your Manus AI assistant
2. Describe what you'd like to change
3. The team will update the content and redeploy

### Contact Information

Update your contact details (phone, email, address) by requesting changes through Manus AI. These appear in the Contact section and Footer.
